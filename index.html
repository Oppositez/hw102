Ayano Tateyama

The Kagerou Project

This Character is apart of a series called The Kagerou Project. The Kagerou Project is a multi-media series that spans light novels, manga, songs, and an anime. Ayano is one of the main characters in the series whose actions greatly impact the rest of the series and it's characters. She is heavily associated with the color red and paper cranes. She becomes friends with Shintaro Kisaragi despite him not wanting any friends. Her adoptive siblings are Kido Tsubomi, Seto Kousuke, and Kano Shuuya.
In many timelines her fate is met with death as a sacrifice for the people she loves. During the first timeline she sacrificed her soul to be turned into a snake to live in Shintaro's mind across everytimeline. This ends up becoming the group's saving grace against the enemy.
